### Topic

Introducing students to Bitcoin open-source development and how they can contribute to the Bitcoin ecosystem.

### Location

Innovation Hub at Cavendish University, Lusaka, Zambia

## Announcement

BitDevs Zambia hosted a stop on the Bitcoin Open Source Software (OSS) Campus Ignition Tour at Cavendish University in Lusaka.

The session introduced students to Bitcoin development, open-source contribution, and the opportunities available for developers interested in working on Bitcoin and Lightning projects.

Participants learned about:

- How Bitcoin open-source development works
- How to start contributing to Bitcoin projects
- Opportunities for students in the Bitcoin ecosystem
